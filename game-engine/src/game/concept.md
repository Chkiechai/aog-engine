
What is the Game?

- The game, called Assassins of Glass, is an interactive text adventure 
game where the player is Addwyn, embarking on her quest 
to kill Lady Locke and avenge her mother. The player will be able to chat
with NPCs, fight, explore, and envision the world of Erylthas.

What entities exist in the game?

- The Player, Addwyn
- Lady Locke, mayor-ess of Two Lakes
- Ophelia, Addwyn's best friend
- Jaidev, Addwyn's love interest
- Addwyn's crew
- The Glass Waste
- Two Lakes
- Waste ships
- Dweller NPCs
- Outsider NPCs
- Addwyn's glass spikes

What does the player do?

- The player will play through the scene of Addwyn's mother's death, then leap forward 10 years and begin
her true quest--the one to kill Lady Locke. She will get on a merchant ship, become friends with them along the way,
fight monsters, get to Two Lakes, and finally kill Lady Locke with her glass spike

ACTIONS INCLUDE:
  - Movement
  - Communication
  - Fighting
  - Observing Surroundings (using the text world)

What is the win condition?

- Addywn kills Lady Locke

What is the lose condition?

- Addywn gets killed

Why did you pick this game?

- I LOVE text adventure games, and Assassins of Glass holds a special place in my heart as a novel I've written. Seeing
it become a game would be one of my life's achievements.
